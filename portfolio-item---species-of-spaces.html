species of spaces

by georges perec

Species of Spaces is a

seminal essay by Georges Perec.

 

As part of my second term at LCC,

I was given the task to interpret Perec's essay in a typographic book.

I created typographic compositions in this book stripped down to the bare basics. Black and white, one typeface, with only the ability to scale, distort and add basic shapes/lines.

Even stripped down to the bare basics, I could still form imagery through typography.
